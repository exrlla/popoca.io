Notion

research - competitor analyisis!

I looked into the current Illinois Design Consulting website and analyzed the strengths and weaknesses of the website. I also looked into other design consulting websites at other campus' to see how they are designed and what they do well!

We had three main takeaways from my team's analysis!

  • As a goal for the IDC Website, we want to increase the University of Illinois' branding. Many of the design websites we looked at lacked in this area, and rather had their own established presence.
  • We want to include community efforts to showcase the community in Illinois Design Consulting, and many of the design websites we looked at do this well by providing images, community links, and blogs.
  • It is clear that many of the competitior websites have different audiences they are reaching. For IDC, we wan't to appeal to our clients, students, and staff and make sure that we include information for all of these audiences such as our portfolio, staff/student biographies, and client information.

Throughout the ideation process, the team and I created a mapping of different pages, branding, and keeping in mind our intended audience. Our Team kept in mind 7 specific groupings in which we thought about areas to improve, and constraints we had with this particular task
